About Us

Mission Statement

"The Urban Foundation's Mission is to transform communities through Collaboration, Research, and Self-Advocacy with young adults."

Our Values

"To be the leading youth-serving organization where all youth are thriving with endless possibilities in Franklin County."

Our Vision


Commitment to the Community - We are committed to ensuring that the youth are integrated into the fabric of the community in which they live, work, play and worship.

Innovation & Creativity -We are committed to researching and developing programming that represents creative solutions to youth and young adult systemic problems.

Fiscal Responsibility - Ensure that the financial resources are used for the best interest of the youth.

Service to Youth and Young Adults - Providing support, mentoring, and guidance for all youth and young adults.

Collaboration -To partner and collaborate with organizations that committed to being agents of change and aligns with the mission of The Urban Foundation.

Excellence -We challenge ourselves to the highest level of learning and performance, tapping the best of the human spirit to create impact for youth and young adults.

Our Founder

Dr. Carl Chastang

Founded 2015

Dr. Chastang, a dedicated veteran from Detroit, is the visionary founder of Urban Foundation Inc. His organization operates with a mission centered around six core principles. Firstly, a "Commitment to the Community" underscores the aim to integrate youth into the fabric of their surroundings. Secondly, "Innovation & Creativity" guides the foundation in developing creative solutions to systemic issues faced by youth and young adults. "Fiscal Responsibility" is a key tenet, ensuring that financial resources are judiciously used for the well-being of the youth. The organization is also dedicated to "Service to Youth and Young Adults," providing crucial support, mentoring, and guidance.

Collaboration is another pillar of their vision, emphasizing the importance of partnering with like-minded organizations to effect positive change. Finally, a commitment to "Excellence" is evident, as the foundation challenges itself to reach the highest levels of learning and performance, leveraging the best of the human spirit to make a lasting impact on the lives of youth and young adults. Through these principles, Dr. Chastang and Urban Foundation Inc. strive to create positive change and opportunities for the next generation.
